Password and Login Recovery FAQ for the AI Knowledge Assistant

How do I reset a forgotten password?
On the login page click "Forgot password", enter your work email, and submit. A reset email arrives within 2 minutes containing a link that is valid for 60 minutes. Open the link, type a new password, and confirm it. You are then logged in automatically.

What are the password requirements?
A password must be at least 12 characters long and include at least one uppercase letter, one lowercase letter, one number, and one symbol. The new password cannot match any of your last 5 passwords.

I did not receive the password reset email. What now?
First check the spam and promotions folders. The sender address is no-reply@assistant.example. If nothing arrives after 10 minutes, confirm you typed the same email used at sign-up, then request the reset again. Repeated failures usually mean the account uses Single Sign-On instead of a password.

How do I turn on two-factor authentication?
Go to Settings > Security > Two-factor authentication, click "Enable", and scan the QR code with an authenticator app such as Google Authenticator or Authy. Enter the 6-digit code to confirm. Save the backup codes shown on screen in a safe place.

I am locked out after too many login attempts. What happens?
After 5 failed attempts the account is locked for 15 minutes to block brute-force attacks. Wait 15 minutes and try again, or reset the password to unlock immediately. Contact support if the lockout repeats, as it may indicate someone is trying to access the account.
