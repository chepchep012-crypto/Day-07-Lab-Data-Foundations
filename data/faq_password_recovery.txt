Password, Login, and Account Security FAQ for the AI Knowledge Assistant

This article explains how to reset a password, what the password rules are, how to enable two-factor authentication, and what happens when an account is locked. It applies to password-based accounts; workspaces using Single Sign-On manage credentials through their identity provider instead.

How do I reset a forgotten password?
On the login page click "Forgot password", enter your work email, and submit. A reset email arrives within 2 minutes containing a link that is valid for 60 minutes. Open the link, type a new password, and confirm it. You are then logged in automatically, and any active sessions on other devices are signed out for safety.

What are the password requirements?
A password must be at least 12 characters long and include at least one uppercase letter, one lowercase letter, one number, and one symbol. The new password cannot match any of your last 5 passwords. Common or breached passwords are rejected automatically, and you are encouraged to use a password manager to generate a unique passphrase.

I did not receive the password reset email. What now?
First check the spam and promotions folders, since the sender address is no-reply@assistant.example. If nothing arrives after 10 minutes, confirm you typed the same email used at sign-up, then request the reset again. Repeated failures usually mean the account uses Single Sign-On instead of a password, so try logging in through your company identity provider.

How do I change my password while logged in?
Go to Settings > Security > Password, enter your current password, then type and confirm the new one. Changing the password signs out all other sessions immediately. If you do not remember the current password, log out and use the "Forgot password" flow instead.

How do I turn on two-factor authentication?
Go to Settings > Security > Two-factor authentication, click "Enable", and scan the QR code with an authenticator app such as Google Authenticator or Authy. Enter the 6-digit code to confirm. Save the backup codes shown on screen in a safe place, because each backup code can be used once if you lose access to the authenticator app. Admins can require two-factor authentication for every member of the workspace.

I am locked out after too many login attempts. What happens?
After 5 failed attempts the account is locked for 15 minutes to block brute-force attacks. Wait 15 minutes and try again, or reset the password to unlock immediately. If the lockout repeats, it may mean someone else is trying to access the account, so enable two-factor authentication and contact support.

How do I sign out of all devices at once?
Go to Settings > Security > Active sessions to see every device and location currently signed in. Click "Sign out all other sessions" to revoke them while keeping your current session. This is the fastest response if you suspect your account has been accessed from a device you do not recognize.
